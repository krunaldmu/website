Museum

Near St Paul's Church is white-walled St Thomas’ Church, a lovely, simple building with genteelely decaying walls. It now houses the Diu Museum, with a spooky, evocative collection of wooden Catholic saints going back to the 16th century and particularly creepy armless angels. Once a year, on All Saints Day (1 November), this is used for a packed-out mass.
