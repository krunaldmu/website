Naida Caves

Naida caves feature a big network of tunnels with square hewn steps, which is yet to be fully explored. It is believed that the Portuguese during their reign hacked building materials from these caves. Visit on a clear sunny day, and be enamored by the natural display of lights created by the caves openings.
